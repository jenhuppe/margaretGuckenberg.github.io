MEDITERRANEAN SEA (Dec. 5, 2016)

View Caption Hide Caption

An F/A-18C Hornet assigned to the Wildcats of Strike Fighter Squadron (VFA) 131 makes an arrest landing on the flight deck of the aircraft carrier USS Dwight D. Eisenhower (CVN 69). The ship, currently deployed as part of the Eisenhower Carrier Strike Group, is conducting naval operations in the U.S. 6th Fleet area of operations in support of U.S. national security interests in Europe.
